- Descarga directa: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/vrm-c/vrm-specification/master/samples/Seed-san/vrm/Seed-san.vrm

- Licencia (según meta VRM): VRM Public License 1.0
  - URL: https://vrm.dev/licenses/1.0/
  - Crédito: **requerido** (meta: `creditNotation = required`).
